Venus

General Information

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is often referred to as the "Evening Star" or "Morning Star." It has unique characteristics:

  • Equatorial Diameter: 12,104 km
  • Mass: 4.87 x 10^24 kg
  • Orbit Period: 225 days
  • Gravity: 8.87 m/s²

Atmosphere

Venus has a thick atmosphere composed mainly of carbon dioxide and clouds of sulfuric acid. The atmosphere creates a strong greenhouse effect.

Surface Features

The surface of Venus includes plains, mountains, and impact craters. Notable features include:

  • Maxwell Montes - The highest mountain on Venus.
  • Aphrodite Terra - A highland region.
