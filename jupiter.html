Jupiter

General Information

Jupiter is the largest planet in our Solar System. Here are some key facts:

  • Diameter: 139,820 kilometers
  • Mass: 1.898 x 10^27 kg
  • Orbit Period: 11.86 years
  • Gravity: 24.79 m/s²

Composition

Jupiter's composition includes various fascinating elements:

  • Atmosphere:
    • Primarily composed of hydrogen and helium.
    • Colorful bands of clouds, including the Great Red Spot.
  • Interior:
    1. Rocky core with a mix of water, methane, and ammonia.
    2. Gaseous envelope surrounding the core.
  • Magnetic Field:
    • Strong Magnetic Field
    One of the strongest magnetic fields in the Solar System.

Rings

Jupiter has a system of rings, though not as prominent as Saturn's rings:

  1. Main Ring:
    • Known as the "Halo Ring."
    • Consists of three main components: the halo, the main ring, and the gossamer ring.
