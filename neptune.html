Neptune

General Information

Neptune is the eighth and farthest known planet from the Sun. It has unique features and characteristics:

  • Equatorial Diameter: 49,244 km
  • Mass: 1.02 x 10^26 kg
  • Orbit Period: 164.79 years
  • Gravity: 11.15 m/s²

Geography

Neptune exhibits interesting geographical elements:

  • Great Dark Spot:
    • An atmospheric storm on Neptune.
  • Rings:
    1. Galle
    2. Le Verrier
    3. Lassell
  • Atmosphere:
    • Methane
    Abundant in the atmosphere, giving it a blue color.
    Hydrogen
    Major component of the atmosphere.
