Mars

General Information

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is often referred to as the "Red Planet" due to its reddish appearance. It has a variety of interesting features:

  • Equatorial Diameter: 6,779 km
  • Mass: 6.39 x 10^23 kg
  • Orbit Period: 687 days
  • Gravity: 3.7 m/s²

Geography

Mars has a diverse landscape, including:

  • Volcanoes:
    1. Olympus Mons - The largest volcano in the solar system.
    2. Tharsis Montes - A group of three large volcanoes.
  • Valleys:
    • Valles Marineris
    A vast canyon system that dwarfs the Grand Canyon on Earth.
