Mercury

General Information

Mercury is the smallest and innermost planet in the Solar System. Here are some key facts:

  • Diameter: 4,880 kilometers
  • Mass: 3.3 x 10^23 kg
  • Orbit Period: 88 days
  • Gravity: 3.7 m/s²

Composition

Mercury has a rocky surface with various interesting features:

  • Craters:
    • Caloris Basin - One of the largest impact basins in the Solar System.
    • Shakespeare Basin - Named after William Shakespeare.
  • Surface Temperature:
    1. Daytime: ~ 430°C
    2. Nighttime: ~ -180°C
  • Atmosphere:
    • Thin Atmosphere
    Primarily composed of oxygen, sodium, and hydrogen.
